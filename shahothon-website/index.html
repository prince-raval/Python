What is Shahothon?

Shahothon is a collaborative sprint for people who love solving real-world problems with technology, design, and creativity. Whether you're a beginner or a pro, this is your chance to learn, build, and present something meaningful.

💡 Ideation

Start with brainstorming sessions, mentor guidance, and clear challenge tracks.

🛠️ Building

Work with your team, ship a prototype, and get support from experts throughout.

🏆 Final Showcase

Pitch your project to judges and compete for prizes, internships, and recognition.
